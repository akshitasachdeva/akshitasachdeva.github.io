About Me

Over the years, I have worked on a series of projects in various fields of research and innovation which include: performing UI/UX Research for the project, Tranquil, a mobile-diagnostic application to detect Depression and Anxiety using Facial Action Coding System; developing Cortana-integrated voice-controlled Windows phone application for the project, Manovue to help the blind and visually impaired access smartphones using their voice; integrating Computer Vision and Text-to-speech conversion in the Manovue device to enable reading of any kind of printed text; implementing Internet of Things to develop the Android phone application for the project, Shwaas, a portable smartphone breath-analyzer that analyzes Blood Alcohol Content and intelligently predicts the time required to get sobered up; integrating Image Processing and Cloud Computing to develop MRaftaar Mobile Phone application that curbs traffic rule violations by generating e-challans in response to the offence proofs submitted by vigilant citizens using the application; developing Konvene Android phone application that makes photo sharing simple, private and automatic using Cloud Computing. My recent endeavour, Griwaditi is an automated, non-invasive and easily accessible Screening tool for Cervical Cancer which uses Image Processing, Machine Learning and Convolutional Neural Networks to detect various stages of Cervical Cancer.
