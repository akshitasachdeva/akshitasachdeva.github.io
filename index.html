About Me

I am a Technology Enthusiast with the vision to empower each and every individual with enhanced, affordable and accessible healthcare using engineering, design and technology. I’ve been working in the field of healthcare innovations since last three years.

My research is focused on Image Processing, Computer Vision and Machine Learning to develop med-tech solutions that enable early diagnosis of chronic diseases and offer digital assistance for incurable ones using mobile and wearable computing.
